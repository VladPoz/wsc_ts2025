FAQ

  • How to rent a bike?

    Find the nearest bike in the app or on the website, scan the QR code and start your ride.

  • Is registration required?

    Yes, you need to register and link a payment method. It will take no more than 2 minutes.

  • Where can I end my lease?

    Parking is available in any permitted location in the city – VelOrda parking is marked on the map.

  • What to do if your bike breaks down?

    Simply end your rental and report the issue in the app. We'll replace the bike as quickly as possible.

  • Is it possible to ride at night?

    Our bikes are available 24/7, and lights and GPS make night riding safe.
